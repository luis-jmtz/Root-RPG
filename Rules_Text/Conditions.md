# Conditions
Conditions are effects that can be applied to creatures through a variety of means and typically negatively affect the target creature.

If a condition has an *X* next to it, it means that the condition can be applied multiple times.

**Condition Immunities**
When you have Immunity to a condition, you completely ignore its effects.

### Bleeding
When you are *Bleeding*, you take 1 True Damage at the start of each of your turns.
- Ending the Bleeding Condition:
  -  Healed: If you heal damage from an effect such as a spell or potion, the Bleeding condition ends.
  -  Medicine Check: A creature can spend 2 AP to make a DC 10 Medicine Check on itself or another creature within 1 space. On a success, the Bleeding condition ends on the target creature.


### Blinded
When you are *Blinded*, you suffer the following effects unless you have an ability that allows you to perceive your surroundings outside of normal sight:
- You automatically fails Check that require normal sight.
- You are *Exposed* (Attacks against you have ADV).
- You are *Hindered* (You have Dis on Attacks).
- If you are not guided by another creature, all Terrain is considered Difficult Terrain.

### Burning
When you are *Burning*, you take 1 Fire damage at the start of each of you turns.
- Ending the *Burning* Condition: You or another creature within 1 Space of you can spend 2 AP to put out the flames. To put out the flames on yourself, you must go Prone. 


### Charmed
When you are *Charmed*, the creature that has *Charmed* you (the Charmer) has ADV on Charisma Checks made against you. Additionally, you cannot target your Charmer with harmful Attacks, Abilities, or Effects.


### Dazed
When you are *Dazed*, you have DIS on Mental Checks and Saves.


### Deafened
When you are *Deafened*, you cannot hear through normal means. As a result you suffer from the following effects:
- You automatically fail Checks that require hearing.
- You gain resistence to Sonic damage, Sonic(x0.5)

### Exhaustion X
When you gain a level of *Exhaustion* you suffer from the following effects:
- You subtract X from all Checks and Saves you make.
- Your Speed and Save DC is reduced by X.
If you gain 6 + levels of *Exhaustion*, you immediately die.
You lose one level of *Exhaustion* per short rest.


### Exposed
When you are *Exposed*, all Attacks against you have ADV.


### Frightened
When you are *Frightened*, you suffer from the following effects:
- You must spend your turn attempting to move as far away as possible from the source of the effect.
- You can only take the Move and Dodge Actions.
- You have DIS on all Checks while you can see the source of your fear.

### Hindered

### Impaired
When you are *Impaired*, you have DIS on Physical Saves and Checks.

### Incapacitated
When you are *Incapacitated*, you cannot Speak, Concentrate, or spend Action Points.
